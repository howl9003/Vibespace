A Planet's Movement Around Its Axis
By John Carlo Narciso

"To all Agerusians. For one, for all, forever..."
-Plumbum, Agerusian Rogue

 The universe was silent. Most races with a conscience and hope, was still and quiet. Sadness was felt in every corner of space. A lush and animated planet, like many others in the whole universe, was going to be invaded by a massive force of a thousand fleets. It was inhabited by Agerusians, and was ruled by one, though this one was a rogue, one who did not conform with the totalistic society of Agerusians...

  Several large metallic buildings built and made from the surrounding mountains, the planet's first and line of defense against an invasion. Each is reinforced with layers of plates, made out of rare alloys found only on a few planets in the whole universe. The only entrance and exits are located at the bases of the mountains, which are 1000 of miles underwater and on top of the adamant fortresses. At the sides of the mountains were several rotating dome turrets made of the same alloys and elements as the buildings. The largest of the fortresses is located at the planet's capital called, the Citadel. Its residents are the planet's top scientists and generals. All the new technology was developed and all the new strategies were devised were created within its thick walls.

  On top of the Citadel, there floated the infamous Agerusian rogue, Plumbum. Like all Agerusians, Plumbum was no different, a sentient being made of planet matter. Though this Agerusian was a revolutionary, one day Plumbum hoped to give all of his kind the choice to form their own government. Agerusians had no specific gender as each one takes roles that can be defined in human terms as male and female, Plumbum decided to take both but his gender was commonly referred by the others as male.
  An Agerusian moves besides him and talks to him in their native "tongue."
  "Sir, the Homeland fleet approaches us," the Agerusian stands there, dwarfed by Plumbum's size, which determines an Agerusian's age. Plumbum is about 3000 years old.
  "Well Juslen, our mother wants us back."
  He turns and looks at the "young" Agerusian, who is two centuries old. The surface of Juslen's "skin" is dark and quite rough, showing that she is healthy and young. Young Agerusians heal and grow quite rapidly that they have rough surfaces, but as they grow old their healing rate slows down, thus they become smoother The wise old giant, Plumbum, is covered with bright and smooth patches. Above him is a small crack and smoothness, a battle scar he received from a battle long ago.
  They move towards the other side of the citadel. Floating quite silently together, a slight buzzing sound grows louder breaking the silence between them. A roar of a million voices is heard as they reach the other end. Below the Citadel, there gathers the planet's populace. Each one similar to the eyes of others, but to an Agerusian each one is unique.
  Plumbum begins to speak and the masses silences.
  "We are Agerusian, and our own kin will strike us," Plumbum closes his eyes in disgust.
  "We are to be annihilated within an hour, unless we conform with them. They are willing to destroy us for the name of conformity. There is a large probability that we will be eliminated, but there still is a chance that we will win."
 Silence once again, as a large shadow begins to covers the Citadel. A doomstar and several destroyers behind it covers the light of the star nearby.
 "As you can see my people, they fear us for we are different. They have even sent M'Sut, the great doomstar. Thanks to our great engineers and mechanics we have created our own," Plumbum happily announced.
  Juslen come forward and gazes at the gathering.
 "Now please evacuate the area, all soldiers report to their battle stations."
 The ground begins to rumble, rock debris begins to roll down the mountainsides and dust begins to rise around them. The gathering moves into the ocean, in an orderly manner, and enters the fortresses through there. Plumbum and Juslen turn and face the other fortress near the Citadel. There the sky roof entrance is opened, and a thousand of Agerusian soldiers spew out of it like a swarm of bees. A hatch opens besides Plumbum. He and Juslen enter the Citadel, knowing that the land and the people they saw will probably be destroyed. Juslen turns and faces the swarm as the whole sky is filled with Agerusian warriors.

 From space, through the enemies eyes, they see that the planet's crust begins to break and move. Others rise, others sink, large parts of the planet breaks away and floats into space. A battle cruiser fleet near the planet loses three ships, as they get hit by several of the planet's parts, now asteroids. The other fleets move away, and others shoot down the asteroids. After a few minutes, most of the planet is now gone, and it reveals its newest form, a planet fortress, quite larger than a doomstar but has limited movement due to its immense size. Surrounding it are several fleets of cruisers and gunboats.
  Only the engines of these starships were heard for a few minutes. A shot was fired, from which side no one knows. From the planet's surface, the whole sky was on fire, like there was another star in the sky. Each shot was a sunspot, each explosion was a solar flare, and for every flame to expire was a death of an Agerusian...
