Untitled
by Jonathan Stearns

"It is forbidden to interfere in Imperial Affairs." The words of his combat instructor at the Xerusian Military Academy were the words that rang through Manorian's head as he crept along the darkened, metallic corridor of the XIG Balthamos.  The ship was in orbit around Pon-138, incidentally Manorian's home planet.  "Everything I know tells me that I shouldn't be doing this."  He thinks, "These "special operations" have never been acts of honor."  Footsteps echo noisily overhead as Manorian slips into a shadow and engages his camouflage.  A heavy sentry, a distinct red glow emanating from his sensor and combat implants lumbers past him, totally unaware of his presence.  As if in an act of fate itself, the atmospheric pipeline over Manorian's head bursts with a hideously loud crack.  "Engage thermal sensors!"  Bellows the sentry.  "Atmosphere this is Dragoon 218 we have a coolant leak on deck..." Manorian's movements are reflexive as he removes an anti-matter blade from his belt and hurls it forcefully into the neck of the sentry, killing him and knocking out his comm.-link instantly.  He carefully drags the body into another shadow and continues quickly down the corridor.  He rounds a bend in the long snaking passageway, then another, and another.  "Eureka." he whispers under his breath as he comes upon an immense cylindrical room filled with plasma relays, atmospheric controls, anti-gravity dampeners, and other unidentifiable objects all clustered in the center on a massive shining spire.  The room is brightly lit and Manorian knows that once he enters it, he will be visible.  Engaging his leg enhancements for speed, and modifying his targeting system to reduce light intake, he rolls sideways into the doorway, leaping to his feet with a noticeable clang.  He gasps almost silently as he realizes his grave error. Directly in front of him, on the cat walk stands yet another heavy sentry.  They stare at one another, steely gaze met with steely gaze.  The Sentry issues a sickening smirk as his right arm, covered in strength implants raises a glistening crystal blade to eye level.  Suddenly, as if from the lungs of some unknown god, words begin to boom against the walls of the immense cylinder.  "You haven't much time." Booms the voice, "Detonate it now! The subjugation of an entire species hangs in the balance!"  Manorian looks back to the sentry, he has heard nothing.  Thoughts and feelings of patriotism and pride swell in his breast as Manorian's hand impacts against the detonator switch adhered to the chest of his combat suit.  He hears the click of the anti-matter trigger engaging, and the room glows white as if the universe itself has become a sun.
