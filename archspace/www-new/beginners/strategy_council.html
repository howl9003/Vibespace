STRATEGY BASICS
 
  Council:

This is another area where we cannot make many suggestions. The choice of how your council will operate is largely in the hands of the players. The important thing to remember is that the council speaker holds the power. They are in charge of all council affairs, although they may choose to listen to any advice as they feel like it. For this reason it can be important to make sure that you are aware of what your council is doing. If the speaker is taking actions you don't like, it may be time to vote someone different into power.
