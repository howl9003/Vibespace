STRATEGY BASICS
 
  War:

This is an area where it will remain to be seen what occurs. The number of options for combat is astounding, so we will only go into the basics

-Siege Planet:
This is the type of attack you use when you are trying to take a planet. You can only siege someone if you are in a state of war with them.

-Blockade:
This is the command you use to blockade an enemies planet. Their defensive forces get one attempt to break your blockade. Otherwise it stays for 24 hours. This will prevent all production from the blockaded planet for the duration.

-Raid:
This is the option you use to cause destruction to your enemies. Your fleets attempt to penetrate their defenses and make a bombing run on their planet. This can often destroy a large number of buildings.

-Privateer:
This is the act of war often known as piracy. It will reduce income of the person affected and give you increased income.

-Defense Plan:
This is an extremely important part of Archspace. You should at least set a generic defense plan. This will then be used for all combats that don't have a special plan listed. You can also put as much effort into special plans as you wish. There are three fields you can alter for special case defense: Race, Power ratio, and Attack type. You can set up defenses for any combination of these three leading to hundreds of possible special cases.

-Battle Report:
This is where you can learn many things. You will be able to download battle report movies from many battles. You can study these to refine your battle strategies. There will also be an option to view a text only report.
