1/22/01 Contact Protocol
 
For the beta period, we will have only one contact email available. This is because at this time, we have an extremely limited Customer Support team. (basically me, but I am also wearing some other hats at this point) We will be improving this later, but at this stage, it is more important that we have more of a tech-oriented team to better fix problems.
So, let me apologize in advance if sometimes you messages are not responded to swiftly, or not at all. I will try to address all sensible messages I receive. I also ask that you limit your content to the following types of messages: Cheating reports (cringe), Questions on game play (only after you have read the beginners guide please), strategies that you feel are very effective (these may not be responded to, but will help us when we begin to improve the beginners guide), and finally, anyone who wants to send money may email me for information. : )

For those of you with suggestions, I ask that you not email them. There will be a forum available for suggestions. We will read this and occasionally ask your opinions. Thank you for your cooperation and I hope you enjoy the game.
