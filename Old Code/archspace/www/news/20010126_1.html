1/26/01 Apache Bug fixed. Also, a Reset
 
Well, after days of around the clock searching, the cause of the bug was found. It has been fixed, and the game should be much more stable now. Unfortunately for some, (but asked for by others) we are forced to reset the server. This is because the old database had too much pre-fix info stored. This made it quite unstable. With the fix, and the reset, the game should be much more stable. Also, many other "bugs" should now disappear, as they were only side effects of the Apache bug. A major one of these is the rollback bug. This should no longer occur.

As for what the reset entails. Your characters have all been wiped. You will have to remake your portal ID. We apologize for this, and we will only reset in major cases such as this. However, in this case, we feel it was more than justified, as the game was almost unplayable with this bug in effect.

Anyways, good luck in the new game, and may the best sentient being win!!!!
