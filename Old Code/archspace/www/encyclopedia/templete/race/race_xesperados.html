The human society is extremely unstable. The masses never seem to be satisfied, and even now, somewhere in their world, a government falls and a new government rises to take its place, destined to yet fall again. Though they live in a society divided by class, (then again, humans never seem to be able to get past this state in their whole history of existence) humans constantly strive fruitlessly for a "better society." Many other species in the galaxy maintain a static society that does not lose or change structure, though power may transfer from time to time. They regard humans disdainfully as "under-developed inferior beings." But the human tendency to never be satisfied with the present is what fuels their rapid growth. Because they are dissatisfied with the present, they want to always strive for a new possibility in the future. Humans readily accept new foreign technology or strong points of other species. This is the reason why they were able to grow in power and become a member of the Supremacy so soon after coming out of the primitive pre-warp stage.
