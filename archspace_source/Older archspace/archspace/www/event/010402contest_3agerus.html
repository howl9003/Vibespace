Racial Picture Story Contest
 

This contest will run for a long time. There will be 30 winners overall.

The basic idea of the contest is this:
There will be one picture posted each week. Each picture will be of one of the ten races. You will then be given 2 weeks to write a story based on the picture.
This story should be a minimum of 1 page and a maximum of 5 and be in English (sorry).

The stories will then be read by our expert staff (maybe even someone who majored in English Literature), and the top three will be selected. These three stories will be placed with the picture in the gallery (that we hope to have soon).

All stories should be sent to: contest@archspace.com

 
Final submission day : May 7 , 2001
 
Final submission day : Apr 23 , 2001
