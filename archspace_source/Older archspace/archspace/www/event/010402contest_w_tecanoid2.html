Technological Surprises
by James Hunt


"This may sting a bit." Those were the words that came through the Medbot's speaker just before the needle was thrust into his bicep. The pain lasted for only a moment before the anesthetic jumped in to counteract. One Medbot went to work attaching the microcomputer to Yutilk's stomach. The microcomputer would enhance Yutilk's reaction time, making his reflexes super sharp, meaning he would be virtually unstoppable in close-quarter's combat. Another Medbot was removing his right arm and began attaching his new bionic arm. This new arm would be as strong as titanium and as quick as lightning. The Medbots continued to work on Yutilk until the operations were complete. He got up off the operating table and flexed his right arm. He could feel the awesome power of his new arm, and he knew that it would prove to be a great asset in the battlefield.

Yutilk left Med Bay 5-679 and proceeded back to his training program on planet K'ulk Kahn 34, where cadets where trained in military procedures in case of an attack from outside forces. Lately, the cadets have been training extra hard because of the Xerusian attack on their neighboring planet, K'ulk Kahn 35.

Yutilk was on his way to K'ulk Kahn 34 when he heard his friend Hyu's voice over his headset. "The Xerusians are invading the south port of Gyrtur! We need everyone now!" Yutilk quickly set his ship's course to Gyrtur. An awesome sight came into view as he neared the city. Thousands of Xerusians were flying in and landing in the city. They were greeted by Tecanoid fighters and cadets. Yutilk flew his ship over the main battle, firing his Gatling rail gun at the incoming Xerusian flyers. The rail gun ripped through the flyer's armor, knocking a few flyers out of the sky. Yutilk landed his ship, and shot out of the cockpit. He activated his body armor and drew his weapon, a Futwick Issue Laser Gun. Time to test the new add-ons, he thought to himself. He ran to the join his comrades at the front of the advancing Tecanoid fighters.

Yutilk's eyes were darting from side to side, watching for Xerusian soldiers. One soldier came running at Yutilk, swinging a sword at his neck. Yutilk's reflexes made him duck at such a speed that it startled him. He then aimed his gun up at the soldier's head and pulled the trigger, killing the soldier instantly. Slightly surprised by the skill in which he handled the situation, he again scanned the battlefield. Another soldier was coming in on his right side, while one more was approaching from his left. Yutilk leapt towards the soldier on his right, performing a flying roundhouse kick that broke the soldier's neck instantly. Before Yutilk's feet touched the ground, his laser gun was out and he fired a shot at the soldier on the left, hitting him in the leg. Stunned by the shot, the soldier hesitated before advancing toward Yutilk again. That hesitation was all Yutilk needed. Moving with incredible speed, he darted to the soldier and broke his neck with one twist of his head. Like a glove, he thought.

More and more soldiers came at Yutilk, but again and again he used superior fighting skills to take them all down. All around him, his comrades were killing Xerusian just as fast, until finally they started to retreat. A mighty cheer rang out among the Tecanoid fighters as the last of the Xerusians flew out of view. The Tecanoid fighters could go home.

On his way back to his home on K'ulk Khan 56, Yutilk heard Hyu's voice once again in his headset. "Nice fighting Yutilk. You wanna teach me some of those moves?" Yutilk replied, "Sure thing, Hyu. I'll see you tomorrow." A smile emerged on Yutilk's face as he traveled off into the sunset.
