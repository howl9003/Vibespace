OBJECTIVE
 

The glory of the powerful Empire that once ruled the whole universe is already dimmed now, and space is entering a long continued time of chaos. Ill feelings are arising between the various species, which lived together and continue to expand to every corner of the universe, and disputes are occurring here and there ceaselessly. The influence of the Empires is still powerful, but its area of influence has shrunk to only a limited area of the central galaxy, and the border areas are abandoned helplessly in front of the rapidly growing local powers. The Empire still wants to keep its influence on these local powers, and continues its effort to restrain them so that no single powerful leader will appear.

You start as the ruler of one planet and the people living on it. With the planet as your base, you will research various technologies to develop your civilization; you can expand your territory by colonizing other planets, too; on the other hand, based upon the high level technologies, you can design and build your own ships and form fleets to enlarge yourself; and through conflicts and cooperation with other rulers, you will be able to survive this age of chaos.

You may be able to discover new possibilities in the course of tech research. You can throw away your material form and become one with the vast universe itself; you can build an ideal society and make a utopia isolated from the tainted influence of the world; you can design a system so complex that it contains knowledge that God may only dream of; you can even create a wholly new universe separated from the one you are now in; and, you can also throw yourself into the bloody world of conspiracy and tricks, and overthrow the old Empire and establish a new order that you yourself is the ruler...

What will you ever be able to find, in this vast universe of Archspace?
