Korrin's Distress
by Korrin

   During the long journey, within Hyperspace, Korrin really had no idea about what to do...or what his next plan of action was?  His future looked really bleak, and he threw back some bitter-sweet Yarinian Wine that was an offering to celebrate the negotiations of Commerce, from the Illuminati Council on Raudnall-(1128.  He laughed and said to himself,  That's right...now I have NO Council.  Why?  Because some 'idiot' wants me dead!!!!  He shook his head and gulped down the last of the wine.

   Seeing that the bottle was empty, Korrin got off his seat and staggered to the compartment that stored the Yarinian Wines, opening yet another bottle.

   He knew he shouldn't be drinking so fast, but the way he was feeling at that moment...he just didn't care.  All he wanted to do was sit there and drink until it either killed him, or he pass out from the heavy consumption.

   He pondered over whether or not he would make it to the Xen'dari Cluster.  By now, word would already be sent, notifying the Emperor that the Assassination attempt failed.  The Imperial Fleets would be dispatched and searching for him.

   Damn!  He thought, Why didn't I take the Commanders advice and upgrade the Shuttles with a Level5-Phase Engine???  Then he laughed at himself, remembering why he didn't,  I wanted to maintain my relationship with the Empire and pay the damn Taxes on time – thats why!!!  Like the obedient, loyal 'git' to the Empire that I am!!!  No, correction – that I WAS.

   KORRIN, the ships computer interrupted his burst of hysterical laughing, VIRTUAL SCAN INDICATES MULTIPLE ENERGY SIGNATURES BEHIND US AT WARP 3.2 AND CLOSING.  ADVISE ON PROTOCOL.

   By the One!!!!  They have found me!!!

   "Computer, Status Report on our current Speed....estimating time until we are within firing range of their weapons." He ordered.

   STATUS OF CURRENT SPEED: AT WARP 6.8.  E.T.A WITHIN: 2 MINUTES, 23 SECONDS.

   That was fast!  The Emperor must have Advanced Technologies that he wasn't sharing, for a ship to jump from WARP 3.2 to WARP 6.8 within a span of 2 minutes.  Korrin was a dead duck.

   He stumbled to the Control Console, and turned on the Rear-Visual Scanners.  A holographic image appear at the centre of the console, displaying what was behind them.  He saw nothing but the endless stream that was Hyperspace, but knew that they were there.  It was just a matter of time before they caught up with him.

   ENERGY SIGNATURES NOW AT WARP 4.3.  ADVISE ON PROTOCOL.

   Korrin peered at the holographic image and could barely make out a distortion.  Puzzled, Korrin knew that the distortion was not any ship he had seen before.

  "Computer, maximise hologram by 2000 on Rear-Visual Scanners."

   MAXIMISED HOLOGRAM: COMPLETE.

   What he viewed, made him turn a deathly, white.  It wasn't the Imperial Fleet....it was ENERGY LEECHES!!!  A whole pod of them!  And they looked hungry!  'Energy Vampires' that siphon off excessive amounts of Energy as it's main food supply.

  Korrin had encountered these foul creatures before, when an Expedition he sent out came back with a pod attached to a Fleet of Dreadnaughts. They did not attack until they were in close proximity of the Home Planet.  And their attack was vicious and without mercy.  It also showed their intelligence and cunning in order to survive.  By piggy-backing onto the Dreadnaughts, they knew that it would returned back to where they could feed.  And Musisi, became it's feeding ground.

   The situation was devastating.  All production on the planet had ceased, as the power plants were shutdown due to the power outage it generated.  But the threat of the ENERGY LEECHES were still there.  News of the Planets plight was sent to the Fleets already in training in a nearby Cluster, and it was they, whom volunteered to divert the LEECHES.  It was their last mission, that saved the Planet.  Out of range, near the edge of the Cluster Territory, the Commander self-destructed the Fleet of DOOMSTARS successfully drawing the ENERGY LEECHES away from the planet and ultimately, destroying them.

  A 'hollow' victory indeed, but it saved an entire Cluster.  And for that, the men and women who selflessly gave their lives so that countless others could live, will forever be remembered.

   Now, it seems that Korrin had the same problem.  The difference being, that he had one 'shuttle' and knew that it wasn¡¯t going to help him much.  He finally made up his mind as he spoke to the Ship's Computer.

  "Computer.  Cancel Hyperdrive co-ordinates and pull us out of hyperspace."

  SYSTEMS ADVISE CAUTION.  THIS WILL CAUSE UNSAFE PROTOCOL AND INTEGRITY OF THE SHIPS INTERIOR.  ARE YOU SURE YOU WANT TO DO THIS?

  "Just do it.  On my MARK.  3.....2.....1.....MARK!!!!

  DISENGAGING HYPERDRIVES: COMPLETED.

  The very fabric of Time and Space stopped, as the shuttle prepared to jettison out of Hyperspace.  Korrin felt like he was turned inside out, and bent in all peculiar angles, and his mind heard a strange whisper of a multitude of voices in his head.  Then it became silent.  The pain became unbearable and Korrin wondered whether or not it was such a good idea to do it.  Then, everything around him began to return to normal.

  "Oh by the One!!!" Korrin moaned, trying to intergrate his psychic, incorporeal form.  "I never want to try that again.  Computer.  Status Report on our current situation?"

  SHIP'S INTEGRITY: AT 56%.  LIFE SUPPORT: STABLE.  MAIN CORE UNIT: STABLE.  SHIELDS REDUCED: BY 65%.  ADVICE ON PROTOCOL.

  "Reroute all power from Shields to Ship's Integrity and..."

  INTRUDER ALERT!!!!  ENERGY SIGNATURE DETECTED ON THE SHIPS HULL....UNABLE TO ....TO....ZZZZZZZZZZZIIIIIIIIIIIIKKKKKK ***

  "Computer!  Reroute ALL power apart from LIFE SUPPORT into the INSANITY FIELD GENERATOR.....NOW!!!"

  REROUTING....ZZZIITTTKK....AVAILABLE POWER....COMPLETED....

  It was a slim chance it would work, but Korrin had no other way to fight them.

  Focusing his psionic powers, Korrin expressed every 'negative' emotion that he was feeling: anger, hatred, fear, into the Insanity Field Generator.  And he began to understand as the rudimental thoughts of the Energy Leeches – no matter how alien – allowed him to cleave a pathway into the alien minds, and therefore, able to fight them.  He screamed aloud his emotions and 'sensed' the pain in the Energy Leeches as they disengaged from the hull of the Ship.  Then they were gone, slipping back into the Hyperspace stream.

  Korrin slumped in his chair, and spoke once again to the Ship's computer.

  "Computer??"he queried, but there was no answer.  "Computer....online."  Still there was no answer.

   The extent of the damage must have been severe.  Korrin, switched over to manual and tried to ascertain his location – to no avail.  Nothing seemed to work and the only thing was Life Support....but for how long?  And where was he?  He didn't recognise the Cluster he was in.

  Assessing what he had, Korrin made the distress message and set the beacon off. The message read as such:  "This is Korrin of Musisi, of the shuttle PSICon1.  I am in need of Assistance.  I have been attacked by Energy Leeches, but the threat is terminated.  My Ship's computer is down and have only Life Support Systems active, but no propulsion to the thrusters.  My co-ordinates are: 847-470-521.  Please respond."

  There was nothing more for Korrin to do but wait.  And it seemed like days before a response came.

  "Korrin of Musisi," a mechanical voice came over the ships communications system.  "You are hereby ORDERED, by Commander Hingis, to LEAVE SAMVARA SPACE.  IMMEDIATELY."

  Korrin was beside himself, from such a display of hostilities.  "You do not understand.  I couldn't leave if my life depended on it!  So, how about some help here people??!!"

  "You have been ORDERED to depart the SAMVARA CLUSTER.  Now, leave or we will be forced to open fire."

  Korrin rolled his eyes and thought what kind of idiot this guy was.  "Have you listened to my Distress Message at all?" he began calmly.

  "Yes." Came the mono-tonal answer.

  "Then you will no doubt know that I can not leave...because my propulsion unit is down.  Now, how do YOU think I will be able to move, let alone get the hell out of your Cluster buddy¡¦WITH NO DAMN PROPULSION UNIT!!!" he shouted angrily.

  There was silence.  The space before Korrin began to distort and his anger subsided when an EVINTOS Dreadnaught uncloaked itself in front of the small, tiny shuttle.  Gulping, Korrin tried to speak, but found that only a small squeak came out.  If he could have spoken, no doubt the words would have been: "F@ck me!!!"

  "Korrin of Musisi," the mechanical voice spoke again.  "Please standby for Commander Hingis's."

  Another voice came through the communications system, but still mechanical.  "Greetings Korrin of Musisi.  I apologise for any inconvenience that may have been caused by this misunderstanding.  We will, of course aid you in your unfortunate position."

  Clearing his throat, Korrin addressed the Commander.  "Thank you Commander Hingis."

  "Please Standby for docking." The Commander continued.  "Our Tractorbeam will guide you in."

  The green rays of the tractorbeam engulfed the shuttle, and Korrin sat back to enjoy the ride.  Once docked, Korrin was faced with rows upon rows of Evintos military, and he had to steady himself in order not to run back into the safe confines of his shuttle.  One of the Evintos came forward and Korrin instantly recognised the Insignia of an Ambassador.

  "Greetings Korrin of Musisi.  I am Commander Hingis.  You are welcomed and an honoured guest of the Evintos Empire."
  Korrin exchanged pleasantries, talking about how he came to such an unfortunate demise.  But he left out the part about being a 'wanted' criminal.  It would have been in bad form.

  Commander Hingis lead Korrin through a serious of corridors before taking the elevator to the Bridge.  Korrin was indeed impressed with the Evintos layout, and technologies he had seen and told the Commander so.

  "Thank you Korrin, we have researchers around the clock, devoting their time and efforts in the search for Advance Technologies.  But, surely you yourself have advanced technologies on Musisi?"

  "Yes we do, in Psionic Power.  But not Advanced Info and E.M.S as what I've seen here." Korrin chuckled.  "You know Commander?  When your Dreadnaught uncloaked itself, I really thought I was going to die."

  The Commander laughed heartily, in his mechanical way.  "We only needed to find out if the distress call was 'genuine' or not.  Lately, we have had....trouble with 'Infiltrators' from other Races as well as the Empire.  But the surface scan showed that you were indeed in dire straights.  I doubt that anyone would go to the trouble to die out here."

  "Well," Korrin smiled.  "By the One, I thank you for coming to my aid."

  As they stood on the Bridge, Korrin once again was amazed by the technologies of the Evintos.  And he whistled in appreciation.

  "Great set-up."  Korrin leaned on the barrister and took in the view.

  "We shall reach our Home Planet very soon....please take a seat." The Commander advised.

  "Thank you Comm....."  A strange sensation began to pulse in Korrin's mind as thousands upon thousands of voices screamed in elation.  "Urrghhhh....."

  "Korrin!" the Commander grabbed Korrin before he hit the floor and settled him into a near-by chair.  "What is wrong?"

  The voices began increasing in pressure and volume and the thought: ASCENSION pounded intensely in his head.  All Korrin could do, was fight until he could strengthen his 'mental shields' to block them out.

  "I-I'm alright....I'm alright." Korrin whispered.

  The Commander eyed Korrin with concern.  "Was that something to do with being a Bosalian?"

  "No....no." Korrin assured the Commander.  "It was displaced 'residual psionic energy'.  Just caught the backlash."  Then Korrin looked at the Commander.  "Tell me Commander.  This 'trouble' you have been experiencing....what was the nature of the threat?"

  The Commander thought carefully.  "We are not sure.  Even our researchers have been puzzled by this strange event.  However, it has taken its toll on our people.  They seem to have lost the need for motivation.  All production has ceased.  We do not know what to do."

  Korrin considered what was said before speaking.  "Yet you and your crew here do not seem to be affected.  Why is that?"

  "It could be that at the time, we were training in a neighbouring Cluster.  Do you have any ideas what it could be?" the Commander queried.

  "Have you ever heard of the 'Whispers of Ascension' Commander?"

  The Commander shook his head.  "Only rumors, but other than that, nothing much.  What is it?"

  "It is a state where a Race attains the Ultimate in Psionic Power.  The Truth of all Truths.  In ages past, it was said that we Bosalians once ventured to attain such 'Enlightenment'.  But somehow......we didn't."

  "But your race is renown for Psionic Abilities.  It should have been easy for you Bosalians to achieve that state." The Commander frowned.

  "We do not understand it either.  Since our failings to attain Ascension, it has had its side-effects.  This body is only held together with the sheer strength of my will.  Lessen my grip even a little than my full strength and my form will become incorporeal."

  "It seems that Bosalians have had a rough deal." He moved the topic on to the original question.  "Will this....'Whispers of Ascension' last long?"

  "I do not know Commander," Korrin turned and a light went off in his head.  "But I think I may know a way in which to help you and your people."

  Korrin discussed the Devices invented by the Bosalian Researchers on Musisi.  The MIND PROTECTOR and PSI-BARRIER.  It was arranged for Korrin to 'insert' the Technology into the minds of Evintos Researchers and within the week, the Evintos now had the means to dispel the Psi-damage the 'Whispers of Ascension' affected on their people.

  Within that same week, Korrin's shuttle was operational and Commander Hingis had the technicians fit a Level 5: Phase Engine with a Cloaking Field Generator added, as a way of his appreciation.

  As Commander Hingis watched Korrin's shuttle depart the planet.  His Domestics Officer spoke to him over the com-link.  "Commander, there is a call from the Imperial Magistrate."

  "Patch him through Salamando."  The Commander replied and waited, wondering why the Imperial Magistrate has suddenly decided to grace them with his ramblings.  Was it more Taxes again?

  "Commander Hingis.  We seek a Bosalian 'Fugitive' by the name of Korrin, formerly of Musisi.  Has any Bosalian been sighted in the SAMVARA Cluster?"

  The Commander paused, then replied.  "No Magistrate.  There have been no signs of any other Race in our Cluster."

  "We shall be in your Cluster within the hour and ...."

  "Excuse me Magistrate," the Commander interrupted.  "But that would be 'unwise' of you.  Recently, we have suffered greatly from the 'Whispers of Ascension'.  We do not know how it will affect others coming into our Cluster."

  "The 'Whispers of Ascension'...." The Magistrate whispered.  "Of course Commander, you are right.  We will quarantine SAMVARA under the 'Interplanetary Quarantine Act,' until the threat 'Whispers of Ascension' has abated.  But remember, Korrin is extremely dangerous and orders are to keep away from him at all costs.  It is 'imperative' that you contact the Imperial Fleets if you come across the 'Fugitive'."

  "Of course Magistrate."  The Commander turned his com-link off and smiled.  Good, he thought.  That would keep Raiders and Infiltrators away for awhile.  Turning to his monitor, he watched as Korrin jumped into Hyperspace.  "Good Luck, Korrin and know that any enemy of the Empire....is a friend of mines."
