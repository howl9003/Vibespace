CATEGORY CONTROLS
 
 

Council:

This category has a central page and five sub links. The central council page contains information and links to the sub links. This information on this page is:
        Council Name
        Council Slogan
        Council Speaker
        Cluster - this lists all clusters that your council has planets in.
        Council Honor- this reflects your council's dealings with other
                                 councils
        Council Safe- this is where PP from donation and taxes are stored         Council Members- this shows numbers of current members
                                     and the max number you can have as
                                     determined by the speaker's social tech
        Council Ranking- this shows your councils ranking
        Achieved Projects- this is a listing of Council projects your council
                                      has
The central page also has a link for the following sub links:
        Council Vote
        Admission Request
        Independence Declaration
        Donation
        Speaker Menu

-Council Vote:
This page shows a list of all the members of your council. It is the page where you select who you would like to support as your leader. You can see who the players in your council are voting for and the total votes that each player currently has.

-Admission Request:
You can use this link to send a message to another council asking them if you can join.

-Independence Declaration:
This page is where you can choose to break away from you current council and form your own.

-Donation:
You can donate a portion of your own savings to your council's safe.

-Speaker Menu:
If you are the speaker of your council you can access this page. From here you can manipulate things within your council and handle intercouncil relations. The options available are:
        Team Spirit:
        This is a method you can use to improve the fleets of your council.
        
        Distribute:
        This will divide the amount of PP you select among your council
        based on power.

        Project:
        This will list any council projects you might be able to buy with
        money from the council safe.

        Financial Aid:
        You can use this to give PP to a specific player in your council.

        Change Council Name:
        You can change the name of your council.

        Change Council Slogan:
        You can change the slogan of your council.

There are also links for the following pages:
        Player Admission:
        You can manage any requests you have received to join your
        council. You can also determine if your council accepts random
        new players.

        Council Diplomacy:
        You can use this link to check your status with other councils, to
        inspect other councils or read and send them messages.
        
        Empire Diplomacy:
        This is where you interact with the empire and deal with the
        demands that they will make.
